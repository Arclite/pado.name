Last Monday saw the release of my first independent app in over five years. [Scrawl](https://appsto.re/us/4PGrjb.i) is an extremely simple app to store quick, temporary notes. These are notes that you don't want to keep around forever, and which probably won't even make sense shortly after they're writtenâ€”parking spots, pizza orders, one-off phone numbers, etc.

Scrawl was written over a weekend after a frustrating experience with airport customs and baggage claims. The 1.0 is extremely barebones; it's simply a text box and a widget that can display text outside the app context.

I've got some ideas on how to make the Scrawl experience a little nicer (special handling for certain data types, automation, etc.), but by its very nature, Scrawl will always be a very basic app. As such, it is available **completely free of charge**; it doesn't even display ads.

Check out [Scrawl on the App Store](https://appsto.re/us/4PGrjb.i) today. If you like it, please leave a review.

**P.S.** Scrawl isn't the only new app I have in the pipeline. I'm hoping to release a slightly more-ambitious app before this year's WWDC. If you're interested in beta testing that app, e-mail me at pado@cocoatype.com.
